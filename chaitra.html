About This Painting

Chaitra marks the beginning of the Hindu calendar year, symbolizing spring's arrival and new beginnings. This painting captures the essence of renewal with vibrant blooms and fresh greenery.

The first month of the traditional Hindu calendar brings with it the celebration of nature awakening from winter's slumber. Delicate flowers burst forth in a symphony of colors, representing hope, growth, and the promise of abundance.

This original artwork is part of a special collection celebrating the twelve months of the Hindu calendar, each piece reflecting the unique character and natural beauty of its corresponding season.
